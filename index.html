Ever wondered what day of the week you were born?

Worry no more, this web application will tell you right that! Yes, the day of the week you were born. There is a bonus to this, we will let you know what they would call you if you were born in Ghana?.

How? You ask

We will take your birthday, calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender output your Akan Name. Don't worry, we don't know your birthday, you'll have to input that. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
